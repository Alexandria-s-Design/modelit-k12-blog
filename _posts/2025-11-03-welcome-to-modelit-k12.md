## How ModelIt! Works

### 1. **Ready-Made, Research-Backed Models**

Instead of starting from scratch, students work with pre-built models of real biological systems:

- **Bacterial Chemotaxis**: Watch bacteria navigate their environment
- **Lac Operon Regulation**: See gene regulation in action
- **Cell Death Pathways**: Explore apoptosis and cellular responses
- **Immune System Response**: Model how your body fights infection

Each model is based on published research and validated by scientists. Students aren't just playing with simulationsâ€”they're **using actual scientific tools**.

### 2. **Interactive "What-If" Experiments**

The magic happens when students ask:

> "What if we add more neutrophils to the immune response?"
>
> "What happens if we block this signaling pathway?"
>
> "Can we predict what the cell will do under stress?"

With ModelIt!, they can **test these questions immediately**. No lab equipment needed. No dangerous chemicals. Just powerful, interactive simulations.

### 3. **NGSS Practice 2: Developing and Using Models**

ModelIt! K12 was built specifically to address **NGSS Science & Engineering Practice 2**:

> "Use models to represent relationships and explain mechanisms of biological processes."

Every activity, every model, every lesson plan is designed to help students:

- Construct explanations based on evidence
- Use models to predict outcomes
- Revise models based on new information
- Communicate scientific ideas through modeling
